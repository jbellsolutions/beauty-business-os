# Social Post Template

> Use this template as a starting point for any social media post. Tell Claude the content type and topic, and it will generate a post using your brand voice.

---

## Post Structure

### Hook (Line 1)
The scroll-stopper. This is the most important line — it determines whether someone keeps reading or scrolls past.

**Hook formulas:**
- "The #1 mistake people make with [topic]..."
- "POV: [relatable scenario]"
- "Stop doing this to your [lashes/skin/hair/nails]"
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."
- "What [service] ACTUALLY looks like vs. what you think"
- "[Myth] is a lie. Here's why."
- "3 things I wish every client knew about [topic]"

### Body (2-4 sentences)
The value or story. Keep it conversational — write like you're talking to a friend.

### CTA (Last line)
One clear action. Don't give multiple options.

**CTA options:**
- "Save this for later"
- "Book the link in my bio"
- "Tag a friend who needs this"
- "Comment [word] and I'll send you the details"
- "Follow for more [niche] tips"
- "DM me [word] to book"
- "Double tap if you agree"

### Hashtags

**Instagram (20-30):**
Mix of broad, niche, and local hashtags. Example:
```
#{{niche_hashtags}} #{{city_hashtags}} #{{service_hashtags}} #{{community_hashtags}}
```

**TikTok (3-5):**
```
#{{niche}} #{{city}}{{niche}} #{{trending_hashtag}} #beautytok
```

*Pull hashtag sets from niche preset or CLIENT_CONFIG.md.*

## Example Posts by Content Type

### Educational (3 examples)

**Example 1:**
```
Hook: Stop sleeping on your face if you have lash extensions.

Body: I know it's comfy, but face-sleeping is the #1 reason lash extensions don't last. The friction crushes and bends your lashes, causing premature fallout. Try sleeping on your back — or at minimum, get a silk pillowcase. Your lashes (and your skin) will thank you.

CTA: Save this for your next lash appointment.
```

**Example 2:**
```
Hook: The order you apply skincare matters more than the products themselves.

Body: You could be using the best products on the market, but if you're layering them wrong, they're not doing their job. General rule: thinnest to thickest consistency. Cleanser → toner → serum → moisturizer → SPF. And yes, SPF is non-negotiable — even on cloudy days.

CTA: Comment ROUTINE and I'll help you build your perfect order.
```

**Example 3:**
```
Hook: 3 things your hairstylist is too polite to tell you.

Body: 1) Purple shampoo is not a daily shampoo — it's a treatment. Use it 1-2x/week max. 2) Box dye makes professional color correction 3x harder and more expensive. 3) Heat protectant is not optional. Not sometimes. Every single time.

CTA: Tag someone who needs to hear this.
```
