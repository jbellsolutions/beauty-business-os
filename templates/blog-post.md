## Post Structure

### SEO Title
```
{{Primary Keyword}} in {{City}} | {{Business Name}}
```

**Title formulas:**
- "Best {{Service}} in {{City}}: What to Expect at {{Business Name}}"
- "{{Topic}}: A Complete Guide from a {{City}} {{Niche Title}}"
- "{{Number}} Things to Know Before Getting {{Service}} in {{City}}"
- "{{Service}} vs. {{Alternative}}: Which Is Right for You?"
- "How to {{Desired Outcome}}: Tips from a {{City}} {{Niche Title}}"

*Keep under 60 characters for search results. Include city + primary service keyword.*

### Meta Description
```
{{meta_description — 150-155 characters including primary keyword and a reason to click}}
```

**Example:**
```
Looking for lash extensions in Austin? Learn what to expect, how to prepare, and why clients love their results at [Business Name]. Book online today.
```

## Internal Linking Strategy

Include 2-3 internal links per post:
- Link to your **booking page** (primary conversion goal)
- Link to a **related blog post** (keeps readers on your site)
- Link to your **services page** (for readers exploring options)

**Anchor text tips:**
- Use descriptive anchor text, not "click here"
- Good: "learn more about our [lash extension services](link)"
- Bad: "[click here](link) to learn more"

## SEO Checklist

- [ ] Primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph, and 2-3 times in body
- [ ] Secondary keywords used naturally throughout
- [ ] City/location mentioned 2-3 times
- [ ] Meta description under 155 characters with keyword
- [ ] H2 subheadings include relevant keywords
- [ ] At least one internal link to booking page
- [ ] Image alt text includes keywords (when images are added)
- [ ] Post is 800-1200 words
- [ ] Content answers a real question people search for
- [ ] Unique, original content (not duplicated from other pages)

## Content Topics by Niche

*Pull from niche preset's `content_themes` if available. General topic ideas:*

- "[Service] Aftercare: The Complete Guide"

- "How Much Does [Service] Cost in [City]?"

- "How to Choose the Right [Service Provider] in [City]"

**Seasonal:**
- "How to Prep Your [Skin/Hair/Lashes] for Summer"
- "Holiday [Service] Specials in [City]"
- "Wedding Day [Service]: A Bride's Complete Guide"
- "[Season] [Niche] Trends You'll Love"

- "Does [Service] Hurt? What to Expect"
- "How Long Does [Service] Last?"
- "Can I [Common Activity] After [Service]?"
- "Is [Service] Worth It? An Honest Review from a [Niche Title]"
