## Monthly Breakdown

### JANUARY: New Year Fresh Start

**Theme:** New beginnings, fresh start, "new year new you" (but make it genuine, not cringe)

**Promo ideas:**
- "New Year, New [Service]" — 15-20% off first appointment of the year
- Resolution package: bundle of services at a discounted rate
- "Start Fresh" new client special

**Messaging:**
```
Start the year feeling your best. Book your first appointment of {{year}} and get {{discount}} off any service.
```

**Timeline:**
- Dec 26-31: Announce on social + email
- Jan 1: Launch
- Jan 25: "Last chance" reminder
- Jan 31: Promo ends

---

### FEBRUARY: Valentine's Day

**Theme:** Love, self-care, treating yourself or someone you love

**Promo ideas:**
- "Galentine's Day" packages (bring a friend, both get discounts)
- Gift cards with bonus value ($50 gift card for $40)
- Self-love pamper package (bundle popular services)
- Couples add-on services

**Messaging:**
```
Whether you're treating yourself or someone you love, we've got you covered this Valentine's Day.
```

**Timeline:**
- Jan 28: Announce promo + gift guide
- Feb 1: Early bird booking opens
- Feb 7: Reminder push
- Feb 10: "Last-minute gifting" push (gift cards)
- Feb 14: Day-of stories and content

### APRIL: Prom / Event Season

**Theme:** Special occasion prep, looking your best for events

**Promo ideas (if applicable to niche):**

## Promotion Execution Framework

For every promotion, follow this timeline:

| Phase | Timing | Actions |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Announce** | 2 weeks before | Social posts, email, stories |
| **Early Bird** | 1 week before | Special pricing for early bookers |
| **Launch** | Day 1 | Full push across all channels |
| **Reminder** | Mid-promo | Re-engage those who haven't acted |
| **Last Chance** | 2-3 days before end | Urgency messaging |
| **Wrap Up** | After promo ends | Results recap, thank you, transition to next |
