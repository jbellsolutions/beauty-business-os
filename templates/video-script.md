## Script Structure

### Hook (0-3 seconds)
The first 1-3 seconds determine whether someone watches or swipes. You must stop the scroll immediately.

**Text overlay:** {{hook_text}} (big, bold, readable)
**Opening line (spoken or lip-synced):** {{hook_spoken}}

**Hook formulas:**
- "Stop [doing this common mistake]"
- "POV: [relatable scenario]"
- "Things [niche professionals] wish you knew"
- "The difference between [cheap option] and [quality option]"
- "Watch this if you [situation]"
- "[Myth] is a total lie"
- "3 signs you need [service]"

### Setup (3-10 seconds)
Give context. What's the problem, scenario, or situation?

**Spoken/text:** {{setup_content}}

### Payoff (10-30 seconds)
The value, transformation, reveal, or punchline. This is why they kept watching.

**Spoken/visual:** {{payoff_content}}

### CTA (Last 3 seconds)
One clear action.

**Text overlay + spoken:**
- "Book link in bio"
- "Comment [word] for details"
- "Follow for more [niche] tips"
- "Save this for later"
- "Share with a friend who needs this"

## Trending Format Templates

### Format 1: POV

```
[TEXT OVERLAY]: POV: You just got your lashes done and you can't stop looking in the mirror

[VISUAL]: Walk up to mirror, do a dramatic reveal, smile/react

[AUDIO]: Trending sound (confident/empowering)

[CTA TEXT]: This could be you. Link in bio.
```

**Variations:**
- POV: Your client sees their brows for the first time
- POV: You finally found a hairstylist who gets you
- POV: It's been 3 weeks and your lashes still look this good

---

### Format 2: Get Ready With Me (GRWM)

```
[HOOK]: "Get ready with me for a full day of [service] appointments"

[CLIPS - timelapse/snippets]:
- Morning routine / getting to the studio
- Setting up the station
- First client of the day
- Working on a service (satisfying detail shots)
- Final reveal
- End of day wind-down

[AUDIO]: Trending sound or voiceover narrating the day

[CTA]: "Want to be in my chair? Link in bio"
```

---

### Format 3: Day in the Life

```
[HOOK]: "A day in my life as a [niche_title] in [city]"

[CLIPS]:
- Alarm / morning (keep it real)
- Coffee / commute
- Studio setup
- Client interactions (with permission)
- Satisfying service clips
- Lunch break (the real version)
- Afternoon clients
- Closing up
- End of day reflection

[VOICEOVER/TEXT]: Brief narration of each clip

[AUDIO]: Trending chill/lifestyle sound

[CTA]: "Follow for more beauty business life"
```

---

### Format 4: Before / After Reveal

```
[HOOK TEXT]: "This transformation is INSANE"

[BEFORE]: Show the starting point (3-5 seconds)
- Lashes: Natural lashes, no makeup
- Skin: Before treatment, close-up
- Hair: Before color/cut
- Nails: Before the set

[TRANSITION]: Use a trending transition effect (hand swipe, snap, blink)

[AFTER]: Reveal the results (5-10 seconds)
- Multiple angles
- Slow motion detail shots
- Client reaction (gold content)

[AUDIO]: Trending reveal/hype sound

[CTA TEXT]: "Book your transformation — link in bio"
```

### Format 6: Storytime

```
[HOOK]: "The craziest thing that happened in my chair this week..."

[STORY]: Tell a short, engaging story while doing a service (or showing B-roll)
- Keep it 15-30 seconds
- Use text overlays to highlight key moments
- End with a punchline, lesson, or heartwarming moment

[AUDIO]: Storytime trending sound or original audio

[CTA]: "Has anything like this happened to you? Comment below"
```

## Production Tips

**Filming:**
- Natural lighting is best (near a window, golden hour)
- Clean background (or intentionally styled background)
- Steady hand or phone tripod
- Film vertically (9:16 ratio)
- Film more than you need — you can always cut

**Editing:**
- Keep clips short (2-4 seconds each)
- Use text overlays for key points (many people watch without sound)
- Add captions/subtitles
- Use trending sounds (check the Reels/TikTok discover page)
- Stay under 30 seconds for highest completion rate (under 15 is even better)

**Posting:**
- Best times: 9-11 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM (test your specific audience)
- Post 3-5 Reels per week for growth, minimum 1 per week for maintenance
- Use 3-5 hashtags on TikTok, 20-30 on Instagram Reels
- Write a caption that adds context or starts a conversation
- Reply to every comment in the first hour (boosts algorithm)
