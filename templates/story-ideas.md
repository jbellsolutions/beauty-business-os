# 30 Instagram / TikTok Story Ideas

> Ready-to-use story ideas with interactive elements for daily posting. Each idea includes what to show and which interactive sticker to use.

---

## Behind the Scenes (Stories 1-6)

### 1. Setting Up My Station
Show your workspace getting prepped for the day — clean tools, fresh linens, products lined up.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Are you a morning person?" (Yes, love it / No, coffee first)

### 2. Product Lineup
Show the products you're using today and why you love them.
**Interactive element:** Question box — "What products do you want me to review?"

### 3. Restocking Day
Film yourself restocking supplies, organizing inventory, unpacking new products.
**Interactive element:** Quiz — "Guess how many [product] I go through in a month?" (give 3 options)

### 4. Studio Tour
Walk through your space and show off your setup — especially if you've updated anything.
**Interactive element:** Slider — "How cute is this space?" (with a heart or fire emoji)

### 5. Between Appointments
Show what happens between clients — cleaning, prepping, snacking, answering messages.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "What's your go-to between-appointment snack?" (Healthy / Junk food, no judgment)

### 6. End-of-Day Cleanup
Show the satisfying process of cleaning and closing up shop.
**Interactive element:** Slider — "Rate my cleanup game" (1 to 100)

## Client Content (Stories 7-12)

### 7. Client Reaction Video
Film the moment your client sees their results (with permission).
**Interactive element:** Slider — "How stunning is this?!" (fire emoji)

### 8. Before and After Reveal
Side-by-side or swipe-to-reveal before and after of today's service.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Before or after?" (rhetorical — the after always wins)

### 9. Client Appreciation
Reshare a client's story tag or review. Add a personal "thank you" message.
**Interactive element:** Question box — "Want to be featured? Tag us in your results!"

### 10. Testimonial Screenshot
Share a screenshot of a sweet text, DM, or review from a client.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Do reviews like this make your day too?" (Yes / Absolutely yes)

### 11. Client Spotlight
Feature a regular client (with permission) — what they get done, how long they've been coming.
**Interactive element:** Question box — "How long have you been coming to see me?"

### 12. Transformation Tuesday / Friday Reveal
Before/after with some context about what the client wanted and how you achieved it.
**Interactive element:** Quiz — "How long did this take?" (give 3 time options)

## Educational / Tips (Stories 13-18)

### 13. Quick Tip of the Day
One actionable tip related to your niche (aftercare, prep, product use).
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Did you know this?" (Yes / No, mind blown)

### 14. This or That (Product Edition)
Show two products and explain which is better for what.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "[Product A] or [Product B]?"

### 15. This or That (Service Edition)
Compare two services and explain who each is best for.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "[Service A] or [Service B]?"

### 16. Myth vs. Fact
Bust a common myth about your niche.
**Interactive element:** Quiz — "True or false: [myth statement]?"

### 17. What I'd Tell My Younger Self
Share advice you wish you'd known when you started in the industry.
**Interactive element:** Question box — "What would YOU tell your younger self?"

### 18. Common Mistakes
Show a common mistake clients make and the easy fix.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Have you ever done this?" (Guilty / Never!)

## Personal / Lifestyle (Stories 19-24)

### 19. Day in the Life
Document your full day from morning to evening in a series of story clips.
**Interactive element:** Poll at the end — "Would you want my schedule?" (Yes / No way)

### 20. Morning Routine
Show your morning routine before heading to work.
**Interactive element:** Slider — "How motivated are you this morning?" (sleepy face to fire)

### 21. What I'm Listening To
Share your current playlist or podcast while you work.
**Interactive element:** Question box — "What's on YOUR playlist right now?"

### 22. Current Favorites
Share 3-5 things you're loving right now (mix of business and personal).
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Which of these should I do a full review of?"

### 23. Honest Chat / Real Talk
Talk to the camera about something real — a challenge, a lesson, a win.
**Interactive element:** Slider — "How much do you relate to this?"

### 24. Weekend Vibes
Share what you do outside of work to recharge.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "What's your favorite way to recharge?" (Option A / Option B)

## Engagement & Promo (Stories 25-30)

### 25. Ask Me Anything
Open Q&A about your services, products, or industry.
**Interactive element:** Question box — "Ask me anything about [niche]!"

### 26. Countdown to Promo
Build anticipation for an upcoming sale, new service, or event.
**Interactive element:** Countdown sticker — set to promo launch date/time

### 27. Flash Promo
Announce a limited-time offer available only to story viewers.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Should I drop a surprise discount?" (Yes please / Obviously)

### 28. Availability Check
Show your calendar for the week and highlight open spots.
**Interactive element:** Poll — "Which day works for you?" (This week / Next week)

### 29. "I Have News" Teaser
Tease an announcement (new service, new location, collaboration, etc.).
**Interactive element:** Quiz — "Guess what's coming!" (give 3 fun options)

### 30. Reshare + Comment
Reshare your latest feed post to stories with a personal caption adding context.
**Interactive element:** Poll or question box related to the post's topic

## Posting Strategy

**Frequency:** 3-7 stories per day for maximum reach
**Best times:** Post throughout the day (stories last 24 hours, spread them out)
**Story highlights:** Save your best stories to organized highlight categories:
- Before/After
- Reviews
- Tips
- About Me
- Services
- FAQ

**Engagement rules:**
- Always include at least one interactive element per story
- Reply to every story interaction (DMs, poll responses, question answers)
- Use a mix of photo, video, and text-based stories
- Keep text readable (large font, contrast with background)
- Add captions to any story with spoken audio
