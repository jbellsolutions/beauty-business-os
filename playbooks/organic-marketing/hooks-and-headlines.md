# Hooks and Headlines Library

> 50+ proven hooks organized by content type. Use these as starting points — customize with your niche, personality, and local flavor.

---

## How to Use Hooks

- **Caption**: The hook is your first line. Everything after "...more" is invisible until they click. Your hook must stop the scroll.
- **Video (Reel/TikTok)**: Say or show the hook in the first 1-3 seconds. Use text overlay so it works on mute.
- **Carousel**: The hook is your cover slide. Make it bold, clear, and curiosity-driven.
- **Stories**: Use hooks as text overlays or opening frames to keep people tapping forward.

## Hook Placement Checklist

- [ ] **Caption**: Hook is the FIRST line (before the "...more" cutoff, roughly 125 characters)
- [ ] **Reel/TikTok**: Hook appears as text overlay in the first frame AND is spoken in the first 3 seconds
- [ ] **Carousel**: Hook is the cover slide in large, bold text
- [ ] **Story**: Hook is the first frame — make them tap to see more
- [ ] **Thumbnail**: If using a custom thumbnail, the hook text is readable at small size
