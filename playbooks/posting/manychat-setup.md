# ManyChat DM Automation Setup

> Automate your Instagram DMs to respond instantly, capture leads, and book appointments — even while you are with a client.

---

## Why ManyChat

When someone DMs you "How much are lashes?" and you do not respond for 6 hours, they have already booked with someone else. ManyChat sends an instant automated reply so no lead goes cold.

Key benefits:
- Instant responses 24/7
- Comment-to-DM automation (massive engagement booster)
- Keyword triggers for common questions
- Welcome flow for new followers
- Free for up to 1,000 contacts

---

## Step 1: Create Your ManyChat Account

1. Go to [manychat.com](https://manychat.com)
2. Click **Get Started Free**
3. Sign up with your Facebook account (ManyChat connects through Meta)
4. Select **Instagram** as your primary channel

---

## Step 2: Connect to Instagram

1. In ManyChat, go to **Settings > Instagram**
2. Click **Connect Instagram Account**
3. Log in to the Facebook account linked to your Instagram Business/Creator account
4. Select your Instagram account and grant permissions
5. ManyChat will verify the connection

**Requirements:**
- Instagram must be a Business or Creator account
- Must be linked to a Facebook Page
- You need admin access to the Facebook Page

## Step 3: Set Up Keyword Triggers

Keyword triggers send an automated DM when someone messages you a specific word.

### Trigger: BOOK
**When someone DMs:** "BOOK" or "book" or "booking"

**Auto-reply:**
```
Hey! So excited you want to book! Here's my online calendar where
you can see availability and grab a spot:

[YOUR BOOKING LINK]

If you have any questions before booking, just ask — I'm here to help!
```

### Trigger: PRICE / PRICES / COST / MENU
**When someone DMs:** "price" or "prices" or "cost" or "how much" or "menu"

**Auto-reply:**
```
Great question! Here's a quick look at my services:

[SERVICE 1] — $XX
[SERVICE 2] — $XX
[SERVICE 3] — $XX
[SERVICE 4] — $XX

Want to know more about any of these? Just ask!

Ready to book? Tap here: [YOUR BOOKING LINK]
```

### Trigger: INFO / INFORMATION / DETAILS
**When someone DMs:** "info" or "information" or "details" or "tell me more"

**Auto-reply:**
```
Hey! Thanks for your interest! Here's a quick rundown:

I'm [YOUR NAME], a [YOUR TITLE] in [YOUR CITY]. I specialize in
[YOUR SPECIALTY].

Here's what I offer:
→ [Service 1]: [1-sentence description]
→ [Service 2]: [1-sentence description]
→ [Service 3]: [1-sentence description]

Want to chat more about what would be best for you?
Or ready to book? [YOUR BOOKING LINK]
```

### Trigger: HOURS / OPEN / AVAILABILITY
**When someone DMs:** "hours" or "open" or "when" or "availability"

**Auto-reply:**
```
My current hours are:

Monday: [hours]
Tuesday: [hours]
Wednesday: [hours]
Thursday: [hours]
Friday: [hours]
Saturday: [hours]
Sunday: Closed

You can see my real-time availability and book here:
[YOUR BOOKING LINK]
```

### Setting Up Triggers in ManyChat:
1. Go to **Automation > Triggers**
2. Click **New Trigger**
3. Select **Instagram DM** as the trigger type
4. Choose **Keyword** trigger
5. Enter the keyword(s) — use "contains" matching so "How much does it cost?" triggers on "cost"
6. Build the reply message in the visual editor
7. Enable the trigger

## Step 4: Comment Automation (Growth Tool)

This is the most powerful feature. When someone comments a keyword on your post, ManyChat automatically sends them a DM.

### Example: "GLOW" Trigger

**Your post caption:**
```
Want 10% off your first visit? Comment GLOW below and I'll send
you the details!
```

**ManyChat auto-DM when someone comments "GLOW":**
```
Hey [first name]! Thanks for commenting! As promised, here's your
exclusive 10% off your first appointment.

Use code GLOW10 when you book here: [YOUR BOOKING LINK]

This code expires in 7 days — grab your spot while you can!
```

### Why This Works
- Instagram's algorithm LOVES comment activity. Every "GLOW" comment boosts your post's reach.
- You capture a DM conversation (now you can follow up with them).
- You move them from a casual scroller to an engaged lead with one interaction.

### Setting Up Comment Automation:
1. Go to **Automation > Triggers**
2. Select **Instagram Comment** trigger
3. Enter the keyword (e.g., "GLOW")
4. You can limit it to specific posts or apply to all posts
5. Build the DM reply
6. Enable

### Comment Trigger Ideas:
| Keyword | Offer | Post Type |
|---|---|---|
| GLOW | 10% off first visit | Promo post |
| SKIN | Free skincare tips PDF | Education carousel |
| BOOK | Direct booking link | Transformation post |
| FREEBIE | Free consultation | Story highlight |
| LASH / BROW / FACIAL | Service-specific info | Service showcase |

### Setting Up Welcome Flow:
1. Go to **Automation > Triggers**
2. Select **Instagram New Follower** trigger
3. Build the welcome message
4. Optional: Add a 5-minute delay so it does not feel instant/robotic
5. Enable

## Step 6: Advanced Flows (Optional)

### Consultation Qualifier Flow
When someone DMs asking about a specific service, walk them through qualifying questions:

```
Message 1: "Awesome! I'd love to help. Quick question — have you
had [service] before?"
  → Button: "Yes" / "No, first time"

If "Yes":
Message 2: "Great! When was your last appointment?"
  → Collect response

If "No, first time":
Message 2: "No worries! Here's what to expect: [brief overview].
Ready to book your first session?"
  → Button: "Yes, book me!" → [Booking link]
  → Button: "I have more questions" → [Tag for manual follow-up]
```

### Rebooking Reminder Flow
Set up a timed message that sends X days after their last interaction:

```
Hey [first name]! It's been a few weeks — how are you liking
your [service] results?

Ready to schedule your next appointment?
[YOUR BOOKING LINK]
```

## Rules and Best Practices

### Keep It Conversational
- Write like a human, not a robot. Use casual language.
- Keep messages short (under 500 characters per message).
- Use line breaks for readability.

### Always Offer a Human Option
- End automated flows with: "Want to talk to a real person? Just say HELP and I'll get right back to you!"
- Set up a "HELP" keyword trigger that notifies you to respond personally.
- Check your ManyChat inbox daily for conversations that need a human touch.

### Do Not Spam
- Welcome flow: 1 message only. Do not send a sequence of 5 messages to every new follower.
- Keyword triggers: 1 reply per trigger. Do not set up drip campaigns that message people repeatedly.
- Comment automation: Use sparingly — 1-2 active comment triggers at a time, not on every single post.
- Give people an easy way to stop receiving messages: "Reply STOP at any time."

### Stay Compliant
- Instagram has rules about automated messaging. Keep your automation within these guidelines:
  - Only message people who initiate contact (comment, DM, follow)
  - Do not send promotional messages to people who have not interacted with you
  - Do not message people who have opted out
  - Keep messages relevant to the conversation they started

### Monitor and Adjust
- Check ManyChat analytics weekly:
  - How many automations fired?
  - What is the open rate on your DMs? (Should be 80%+)
  - How many leads came through comment automations?
  - Which keyword triggers are used most?
- Adjust messages based on what is working. If people are not clicking your booking link, try rewording the CTA.
