# Posting Agent — Auto-Posting Workflow

> How the content-to-post pipeline works, from creation to publishing.

---

## The Posting Pipeline

```
Content Created (batch day)
  → Edited (CapCut / InShot / Canva)
    → Caption written (hook + body + CTA + hashtags)
      → Reviewed and approved by you
        → Scheduled in Metricool
          → Auto-posted to connected platforms
```

Every piece of content flows through this pipeline. Nothing gets posted without review. The goal is to batch everything on Sunday so the rest of the week runs on autopilot.

## Optimal Posting Times by Platform

| Platform | Best Time (General) | Secondary Time | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instagram Feed | 5-7 PM local | 11 AM-1 PM | Check Insights > Audience > Most Active |
| Instagram Reels | 6-8 PM local | 9-11 AM | Reels can perform well at any time due to Explore |
| Instagram Stories | Throughout the day | Morning + evening | Space out 3-5 stories across the day |
| TikTok | 6-9 PM local | 11 AM-1 PM | Check Analytics > Followers > Active Times |
| Facebook | 9-11 AM local | 1-3 PM | Weekdays perform best |

**Always override general times with your own analytics.** After 2-3 weeks of consistent posting, your insights will tell you exactly when your audience is online.

## Cross-Posting Strategy

Film once. Edit for each platform. Each platform has different specs and culture.

### From One Video, Create:

| Platform | Format | Specs | Edit Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| TikTok | Vertical video | 9:16, 1080x1920 | Raw feel, trending sound, text overlays, TikTok-native captions |
| Instagram Reel | Vertical video | 9:16, 1080x1920 | Slightly more polished, Instagram music, branded text |
| Instagram Feed | Square or 4:5 crop | 1080x1350 (4:5) or 1080x1080 | Cover image with hook text, carousel option |
| Instagram Story | Vertical video | 9:16, 1080x1920 | Add polls, questions, links, or "new post" CTA |
| Facebook | Square or vertical | 1080x1080 or 9:16 | Same as IG Reel or separate text-based post |

### Cross-Posting Rules
- **Never post a TikTok with the TikTok watermark to Instagram.** Instagram suppresses watermarked content from other platforms. Save the original video before posting to TikTok, or use a watermark remover.
- **Adjust captions for each platform.** TikTok captions are short (1-2 sentences). Instagram captions can be long. Facebook posts are conversational.
- **Stagger posting times.** Do not post to all platforms at the same moment. Space them out by 1-2 hours.
- **TikTok-first for trends.** If you are doing a trending sound or format, post to TikTok first (where trends originate), then adapt for Instagram 1-2 days later.

## Scheduling Cadence

### Sunday: Schedule the Entire Week

1. Open Metricool (or your scheduling tool)
2. Upload all content for the week (already edited and captioned from batch day)
3. Set posting times based on your analytics
4. Double-check:
   - [ ] Captions have hooks in the first line
   - [ ] Hashtags are included and rotated (not the same set every day)
   - [ ] Location is tagged
   - [ ] CTA is clear on every post
   - [ ] Cover images are set for Reels (if applicable)
5. Enable auto-post for Instagram and Facebook
6. Set reminders for TikTok manual posts (manual posting gets better reach than auto-posting on TikTok)

### Daily: Quick Check (5 minutes)

- Confirm scheduled post went live
- Engage for 15-20 minutes after it posts (respond to comments, engage with others)
- Post 2-3 stories organically throughout the day (these should NOT be pre-scheduled — they should feel real-time)

### Weekly: Review (15 minutes)

- Check which posts performed best
- Note any content ideas from comments or DMs
- Adjust next week's plan based on performance

## Content Approval Checklist

Before any post goes into the scheduling queue:

- [ ] Hook is compelling (would YOU stop scrolling for this?)
- [ ] Content delivers value (educates, entertains, inspires, or sells)
- [ ] CTA is clear (what do you want them to do after consuming this?)
- [ ] Image/video quality is good (clear, well-lit, in focus)
- [ ] Branding is consistent (colors, fonts, tone of voice)
- [ ] Caption is formatted correctly (hook, body, CTA, hashtag break, hashtags)
- [ ] Hashtags are appropriate and rotated
- [ ] Location is tagged
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Client consent obtained for any client photos/videos
- [ ] Post is scheduled for the optimal time
