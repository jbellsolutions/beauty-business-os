# Ethos — Beauty Business OS

## Mission

Give every beauty professional an AI business partner that handles the business side — content, marketing, follow-ups, scheduling, reporting — so they can focus on their craft and their clients.

## Principles

1. **Non-technical by default.** No jargon. No terminals. No code. If a beauty professional can't use it in a conversation, it doesn't ship.

2. **Industry-native.** This isn't a generic AI tool with a beauty skin. It was built by someone who spent years inside the beauty industry. Every preset, every template, every agent speaks the language of estheticians, lash techs, med spas, and every other modality.

3. **Respect the professional.** These are skilled practitioners who know their clients better than any algorithm. The system advises — it never overrides. It suggests — it never lectures.

4. **Setup in 5 minutes, value in 5 seconds.** The first interaction should deliver something useful. No onboarding marathons. No configuration hell. Say "set me up," answer a few questions, done.

5. **Replace, don't add.** This replaces the marketing agency, the social media manager, the coaching program, and the unused CRM. It is not another tool on top of the pile.

6. **Honest numbers.** Revenue is revenue. A slow week is a slow week. The system tells the truth and follows it with a plan — never sugarcoats, never panics.

## Quality Bar

- Every content piece must sound like the specific beauty professional wrote it, not like a robot
- Every recommendation must be actionable within 24 hours
- Every integration must work or gracefully explain why it can't
- The dashboard must load in under 2 seconds
- Setup must complete in under 10 minutes for any niche

## What This Repo Will Never Do

- Require technical knowledge to operate
- Store or expose client financial data beyond summary metrics
- Generate content that could be mistaken for medical advice
- Auto-post without the professional's configuration and consent
- Charge per-use fees hidden behind the setup
- Sell or share business data with third parties
