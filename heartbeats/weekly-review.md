# Weekly Review Heartbeat

**Trigger:** Sunday evening, or when user says "how was my week" / "weekly review" / "weekly report"

## Data Sources
1. **GHL:** Weekly revenue, bookings, new leads, pipeline
2. **Content:** Posts this week, engagement metrics
3. **Previous week data:** For comparison
4. **Niche preset:** Industry benchmarks for context

## Output Format
Comprehensive but scannable. This is the one time per week they get the full picture.

1. **Revenue** — This week vs last week, with percentage change
2. **Bookings** — Count, average value, top service
3. **Content** — Posts published, top performer, engagement
4. **Leads** — New leads, conversion rate
5. **3 Recommendations** — Specific, actionable things to do next week

## Example Output
```
📊 WEEKLY REVIEW — Glow Studio

💰 REVENUE
This week: $2,150
Last week: $1,800
Change: +19% 📈 — your best week yet!

📅 BOOKINGS: 14 services
Avg value: $154
Top service: Volume Full Set (5 bookings)
New clients: 3

📱 CONTENT: 5 posts this week
🏆 Top post: Lash lift timelapse — 1,200 views, 45 saves, 8 comments
That timelapse format is working. Let's do more of those.

👥 LEADS: 6 new inquiries → 4 booked (67% booking rate — excellent!)

🎯 NEXT WEEK — 3 RECOMMENDATIONS

1. DOUBLE DOWN ON TIMELAPSE — Film one for every appointment this week. Your lash lift video outperformed everything. This is your content format.

2. REBOOK PUSH — 3 clients from 2 weeks ago haven't rebooked. Send a quick "miss your lashes!" DM Tuesday.

3. MOTHER'S DAY PREP — It's 3 weeks out. Create a "Treat Mom" gift certificate package this week and start promoting. Lash lift + brow lamination bundle = perfect gift.

Amazing week! You're building real momentum. 🚀
```
