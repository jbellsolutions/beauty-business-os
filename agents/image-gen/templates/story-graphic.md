# Story / Reel Cover Graphic Template

## Overview
Generates vertical background graphics for Instagram/TikTok Stories and Reel covers. These are the everyday workhorse graphics — used for quick announcements, tips, Q&A prompts, and engagement content. The AI creates the background; the user adds text.

## Dimensions
- **Primary:** 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- All story and reel cover content uses this single dimension

## Layout Spec
- Bold headline area: upper third (largest text zone)
- Brand element: top corner or subtle watermark
- CTA area: bottom fifth ("Swipe up", "Book now", "Reply to this story")
- Large open space in the middle for text overlay
- Visually interesting but not busy — text readability is priority

## Content Types

### 1. Appointment Reminder
- Calm, professional background
- Space for: "Your appointment is tomorrow!" + time + prep instructions
- Soft, reassuring aesthetic

### 2. Tip of the Day
- Educational, approachable feel
- Space for: headline tip + 2-3 bullet points
- Example: "3 Things to Do Before Your Lash Appointment"

### 3. Q&A Prompt
- Engaging, interactive feel
- Space for: "Ask me anything about [service]!"
- Works with IG's question sticker overlay

### 4. Poll Background
- Fun, playful aesthetic
- Space for: question + two options
- Works with IG's poll sticker overlay

### 5. Countdown / Coming Soon
- Exciting, anticipatory energy
- Space for: event/offer name + date
- Works with IG's countdown sticker

## Post-Generation Workflow
1. AI generates the story background
2. User opens in Instagram Stories editor or Canva
3. User adds:
   - Text (headline, body, CTA)
   - Interactive stickers (poll, question, countdown, quiz)
   - GIFs or additional elements
   - Music (for video stories)
4. Post to Stories or save as Reel cover

## Batch Generation
Story graphics are used daily, so batch generation is efficient:
- Generate 5-7 backgrounds at once in different styles
- Save to a "Story Backgrounds" folder
- Grab one each day and add fresh text
- Rotate styles throughout the week

**User says:** "I need a story graphic for a lash tip"

- Suggested sticker: quiz ("True or False: You can wear mascara with extensions")

**User says:** "Make me a Q&A story background"

- The generated image

## Best Practices
- Keep text to 5-7 words for headlines (stories are glanced at, not read)
- Use high contrast between background and text
- Maintain consistent brand look across all story graphics
- Post 3-5 stories per day for maximum reach
- Mix content types: 60% value/tips, 20% personal/behind-scenes, 20% promo
