# Opt-In Page Copy Template

> This template generates the lead capture opt-in page. Swap in your lead magnet details and niche-specific content.

---

## Page Content

### Section 1: Hero / Above the Fold

**Headline:**
```
FREE: {{lead_magnet_title}}
```

**Subheadline:**
```
{{lead_magnet_description_one_liner}}
```

**Image:** Mockup of the guide/checklist, or a relevant lifestyle/service image

---

### Section 2: Benefit Bullets

**What you'll learn:**

- {{benefit_1}} (e.g., "The #1 mistake that ruins your lashes in the first week")
- {{benefit_2}} (e.g., "The exact products pros recommend for aftercare")
- {{benefit_3}} (e.g., "How to make your results last twice as long")
- {{benefit_4}} (e.g., "A printable daily routine you can stick on your mirror")

*Keep bullets specific and outcome-focused. "Learn how to X" is weaker than "The exact method to X."*

---

### Section 3: Opt-In Form

**Form Fields:**
- First Name (required)
- Email Address (required)
- Phone Number (optional — label: "Get text reminders too (optional)")

**Submit Button:**
```
Send Me the Free {{lead_magnet_type}} →
```

*Button should be large, high-contrast, and use action language.*

---

### Section 4: Trust Elements

**Below the form:**
```
🔒 No spam, ever. Unsubscribe with one click.
```

**Social proof:**
```
Join {{download_count}}+ beauty lovers who already grabbed this!
```

*Start with a reasonable number (500+) and update as actual downloads grow.*

---

### Section 5: About (Below the Fold)

**Short bio:**
```
Hi! I'm {{provider_name}}, a {{niche_title}} in {{city}}. I created this free {{lead_magnet_type}} because {{reason_why}} — and I wanted to share what I've learned with anyone who needs it.
```

---

## Design Notes

- **Layout:** Single column, centered, minimal distractions
- **No navigation bar:** Remove all other links — the only action is to opt in
- **Mobile-first:** Large tap targets, form fills easily on phone
- **Colors:** Brand primary as CTA button color, clean white or light background
- **Speed:** Keep the page lightweight — under 3 seconds load time
- **Form placement:** Visible without scrolling on mobile (above the fold)

## Lead Magnet Delivery

After form submission:
1. Redirect to thank-you page with download link
2. Send welcome email with download link (backup delivery)
3. GHL: Create contact, apply tags, trigger nurture workflow

## A/B Testing Ideas

- **Headline:** Test benefit-driven vs. curiosity-driven headlines
- **Button text:** "Get My Free Guide" vs. "Send It to Me" vs. "Download Now"
- **Image:** Mockup vs. lifestyle photo vs. before/after
- **Form length:** With phone vs. without phone (shorter form = higher opt-in, but phone enables SMS)
- **Social proof number:** Test different proof points
