# Beauty Business OS — Walkthrough

## What This Is

An AI-powered business operating system for beauty professionals. You talk to it like a business partner — it handles content, marketing, follow-ups, scheduling, and reporting for your beauty business.

## Prerequisites

- **Claude Code** — Download from [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download) (Mac or Windows)
- That's it. No other software, accounts, or technical knowledge required.

## Optional Integrations

These make the system more powerful but are NOT required:

| Integration | What It Does | How to Get It |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| GoHighLevel | CRM — leads, bookings, campaigns | Your GHL API key |
| Metricool | Auto-post to social media | Free signup at metricool.com |
| Replicate | AI image generation (~$0.01/image) | Free signup at replicate.com |
| Google Calendar | Appointment sync | Connect during setup |
| ManyChat | Instagram DM automation | Free signup at manychat.com |

## Setup (5 Minutes)

1. Open Claude Code (the app you downloaded)
2. Open this `beauty-business-os` folder
3. Say **"set me up"**
4. Answer 10-15 questions about your business (name, niche, services, pricing, brand voice)
5. The AI configures everything — you're live

## Your First Commands

After setup, try these:

| Say This | Get This |
|----------|----------|
| "What's on my schedule today?" | Morning briefing with appointments, tasks, revenue |
| "Create this week's content" | 5-7 social posts in your brand voice |
| "Make me a promo for my lash special" | Promo graphic + caption, ready to post |
| "How much did I make this week?" | Revenue summary |
| "What should I post today?" | Content idea for your niche |
| "Follow up with yesterday's leads" | Automated follow-up messages |
| "Plan my content for next week" | Full content calendar |

## Daily Workflow

- **Morning:** "What's on my plate today?" — get your briefing
- **Between clients:** "Post that reel I filmed yesterday at 6pm" — schedule content
- **End of day:** "How did I do today?" — daily summary
- **Sunday:** "Plan my content for next week" — batch everything

## Dashboard

Open `dashboard/index.html` in your browser for a visual overview of appointments, revenue, content, leads, and tasks.

## Customization

- Change your brand voice anytime: "Change my voice to professional"
- Update services: "Add microneedling at $250"
- Switch niches: "I'm also doing body contouring now"
